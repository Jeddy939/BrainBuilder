How to Play

Click Add Neurons or build factories to grow your neuron count.

Spend neurons on upgrades to unlock new features and boost production speed.

Psychbucks come from factories and completed projects and pay for advanced upgrades.

Ops (operations) are generated every time neurons are created – you gain 0.01 Ops per neuron from clicking or factories. Ops power special projects.

Manual and passive neuron creation both consume Fuel. Buy food with Psychbucks to keep your fuel tank full.
